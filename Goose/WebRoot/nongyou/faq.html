Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive a response to my booking?
The booking confirmation is given INSTANTLY because room availability is constantly updated in the system. In any case each individual hotel is solely responsible for everything concerning booking management through the system. When hotels join the network, they undertake to constantly update their data on room availability.

What is the payament method?
The price of requested room is confirmed by HR Hotel Reservation. A deposit is required at the time of booking with remainder payable on checkout. Your credit card is charged for the cost indicated at time of purchase. Prices and rooms availability are not guaranteed until the online payment is received. The deposit is not refoundable.

Safety/Privacy
The information requested during registration is necessary for the hotel and reXervation to manage the booking request. All information is strictly confidential and shall be transmitted via an encrypted channel that ensures privacy. Your credit card data, necessary to guarantee booking, is also transmitted through safe, on the protected bank server (SSL Secure Server).

How do I cancel a reservation?
If you wish to cancel your reservation for any reason you must telephone your Hotel number before the time indicated by the Hotel in the section "cancellation policy". Be ready to quote the information contained in your Reservation Confirmation. If you cancel your reservation within the days indicated by the Hotel in the Cancellation Policy Section of the Reservation Confirmation we will not debit your credit card for the first night, thereby forfeiting your deposit. If you cancel your reservation after the days indicated by the Hotel in the Cancellation Policy Section of the Reservation Confirmation the hotel have the right to make a No Show Charge on your credit card for the total price of first night reserved. Cancellation is not valid by any other method. Cancellation of the reservation will result in forfeiture of the deposit taken at the time of booking. Please note that we are unable to give a refund if you return early. If you have any questions or complaints about your prepaid reservation, please contact our call center.

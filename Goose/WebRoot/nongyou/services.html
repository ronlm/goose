Food & Beverage
There has very good cultural meaning in the names of the hotel’s compartments. Every one has unique cultural overtone. In the field of food & beverage culture, the hotel has basically inherited Chinese traditional historical culture. The style mainly relies on the culture of Tang Dynasty and Song Dynasty. It will give you the beautiful enjoyment. While tasting the delicious food, our guests can also have a taste of our country’s abundant cultural intension.

1. Saint-lorens Western Restaurant
　　 The atmosphere there is mostly exotic and romantic. It is noble and succinct, serving coffee, drinks, western food of English style and French style and buffet meals. You can be addicted to the relaxed and happy atmosphere, and taste different kinds of Continental or Asian delicacies. It might be the best place for relatives and friends to have their get-together.

2. Wen Hua Court
　　 On the 2nd floor lies the gorgeous and graceful banqueting hall with an atmosphere of culture and art, where you can enjoy the different delicious food from all over the country. 18 graceful Chinese food compartments with various styles offer lunch and dinner service every day.

3. Dao Xiangcun Cuisine Street
　　 The special Chinese national style can be seen here from the thoughtful classical and graceful decoration. There have a special flavour food street which suits salariat’s consumption level, a banquet hall and three private rooms.

4. Park View Café
　　 On the 25th floor is the Park View café, where you can enjoy coffee, cocktails, either hot or cold drinks and exquisite pastries. You can also get a bird’s-eye view of the Nanyang city, which is both old and modern and famous for its history and culture.

5. Sakura
　　 The style is different, warm and graceful. Here you can enjoy the table delicacies from foreign country. And it will bring you the genuine Japanese tastes!
